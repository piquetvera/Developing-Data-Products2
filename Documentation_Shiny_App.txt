

Instructions For Running the Application
1. The Data that the application uses is from the MTCARS dataset in Rstudio
2. To Launch the Application, click on the following link. It may be advisable to right click and "open in new window" 
 https://piquetvera.shinyapps.io/final_project_data_products/
3. Once at the shinyapps.io website you will see a panel of optional variables on the right. 
4. To make the application render the associated plots, you must select 2 or more optional variables.
5. Selecting less than one variable will not do anything.
